Styling Classes

Styling a Component

Timber comes with a range of styling classes that can be used for components to quickly change their appearance depending on the section in which they appear. The extent to which they can be customized using these styling classes depends on their structural complexity. For the more complex compoents it is adviced to use CSS to overwrite base classes.

The component editor below suggests what styling classes work best with what component and gives the corresponding HTML that can be copied and pasted into your project.
