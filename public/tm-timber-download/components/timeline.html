Timeline

Showing Important Events

Timelines are a nice and easy way to show a representation of a period of time on which important events are marked. Below is an example of a simple timeline vertical timeline.

  • 2007 - 2008

    Massachusetts Institute of Technology

    Co-wrote a book on the Semantic Web and Best Practices for Developers. Awarded the MIT entreprenur of the year (elected by student body).

  • 2004 - 2006

    Parsons School of Design

    Awarded Best Designer of the Year voted by student and teacher body.

  • 2002 - 2004

    Berkley College

    Two year program with a focus on design principles and Javascript development. Won National Student Design Competition for Application Design.
